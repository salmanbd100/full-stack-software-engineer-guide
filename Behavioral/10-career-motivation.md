# Career Goals & Motivation

## Overview
Career and motivation questions assess your professional aspirations, what drives you, cultural fit, and long-term potential with the company. These questions help interviewers understand if your goals align with the role and organization, and whether you'll be engaged and committed.

## ğŸ“‹ Common Questions

### Career Goals
1. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
2. What are your short-term and long-term career goals?
3. What's your ideal next role?
4. How does this position fit into your career plan?
5. What skills do you want to develop?
6. What does career success look like to you?

### Motivation & Passion
1. Why do you want to work here?
2. What motivates you in your work?
3. What excites you about this role?
4. Why are you leaving your current job?
5. What makes you passionate about technology?
6. What kind of work environment brings out your best?

### Values & Culture Fit
1. What's most important to you in a company?
2. Describe your ideal work culture
3. What are your core professional values?
4. What type of manager do you work best with?
5. How do you define success?

### Company Knowledge
1. What do you know about our company?
2. Why this company over competitors?
3. What product or feature of ours do you find most interesting?
4. Where do you see our company going?
